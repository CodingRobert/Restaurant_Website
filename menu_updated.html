Menu

Margherita Pizza — $10 Our Margherita pizza is a classic masterpiece made with the freshest, authentic ingredients. A thin, crispy handmade crust forms the perfect base, topped with rich, tangy San Marzano tomato sauce. Creamy, melted mozzarella di bufala blankets the pizza, creating a smooth, delicate texture. Fresh basil leaves add a fragrant, herbal note that perfectly balances the flavors. Every bite bursts with the simplicity and harmony of genuine Italian tradition—fresh, natural, and unpretentious. Perfectly baked to golden perfection, this pizza offers a delightful mix of savory, creamy, and aromatic tastes that celebrate true quality and craftsmanship in every slice.
Spaghetti Carbonara — $12 Our Pasta Carbonara features perfectly al dente spaghetti tossed in a creamy sauce made from fresh eggs, cheese, and crispy guanciale. The smoky, savory bites of guanciale complement the rich, velvety sauce, creating a balanced flavor that’s both comforting and indulgent. Finished with a touch of black pepper, this traditional Roman dish highlights the beauty of simple, high-quality ingredients prepared with care and authenticity.
Caesar Salad — $8 Our Caesar Salad starts with crisp, fresh lettuce, hand-selected for its vibrant crunch and bright green color. Tossed in a creamy, perfectly balanced dressing made from a blend of anchovies, freshly squeezed lemon juice, garlic, Dijon mustard, and aged Parmesan cheese, it delivers a burst of savory and tangy flavors in every bite. Golden, crunchy garlic croutons add a delightful texture contrast, while extra shavings of Parmesan finish the dish with a rich, nutty depth. This salad is a harmonious blend of fresh, bold, and satisfying ingredients that come together for a timeless, refreshing experience.
Tiramisu — $6 Our Tiramisu is a decadent layered dessert that combines delicate ladyfingers soaked in rich, freshly brewed espresso and a subtle touch of sweet liqueur. These layers are perfectly balanced with a creamy, velvety mascarpone mixture, whipped to a light yet indulgent texture. Each bite offers a harmonious blend of bold coffee bitterness and smooth sweetness, enhanced by a dusting of fine cocoa powder on top. Crafted with the finest ingredients, this dessert delivers a luxurious, melt-in-your-mouth experience that feels both elegant and comforting, making it the perfect ending to any meal or a delightful treat on its own.
